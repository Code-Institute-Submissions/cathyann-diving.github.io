Diving in Malaysia

Malaysia has long been a notable destination for diving,
renowned the globe over for clear waters and diverse marine life.
Malaysia is fast becoming one of the leading dive destination
of the world with the richest marine environment in the Indo-Pacific Basin.
The incredible bio-diversity of marine life coupled with beautiful islands
with white sandy beaches and clear warm waters keep the divers coming back for more.
From schooling hammerhead sharks, huge schools of barracudas and various turtles
to the bizarre frogfish or ghost pipefish, there is always something to fascinate the diver.
